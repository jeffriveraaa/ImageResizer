How to use Image Resizer?

Step 1

Click "Choose File" button and select the picture you want to resize.

Step 2

Use the input boxes to set your desired size for the attached image.

Step 3

Hover or click your mouse in download button to save your image as jpg, png, gif, or webp file.

Done!
